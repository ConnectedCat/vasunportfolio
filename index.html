For more than a decade Vasun has worked across the disciplines of design, art direction, typography, and culture to produce compelling experiences across diverse touchpoints. She has crafted and managed brand systems, and seeks opportunities that redefine the role of a designer. She thrives in a team culture of inclusivity and collaboration and fosters diverse perspectives that challenge existing norms and cultivate innovative approaches.

Previously, Vasun was Global Design Director at Hypebeast, and has worked with a range of studios such as Office of Paul Sahre, MoMA Design Studio, Ralph Appelbaum Associates in New York City, and Public-Library, Commonwealth Projects, IN-FO.CO and Rapt Studio in Los Angeles.

Vasun received her MFA from Cranbrook Academy of Art and her undergraduate degree from Parsons, The New School for Design.
