Vasun Pachisia
Los Angeles, CA

Vasun is an Indian-born, LA based designer and a Design Director at Hypebeast. She has designed and managed brand systems, and crafted compelling experiences across diverse touchpoints. She seeks opportunities that redefine the role of a designer and actively explores ways to push the boundaries of design. She thrives in a team culture of inclusivity and collaboration and fosters diverse perspectives that challenge existing norms and cultivate innovative approaches.

Vasun received her MFA from Cranbrook Academy of Art in 2016 and her undergraduate degree from Parsons, The New School for Design. Vasun has worked with a range of studios such as Office of Paul Sahre, MoMA Design Studio, Ralph Appelbaum Associates in New York City, and Public-Library, Commonwealth Projects, IN-FO.CO and Rapt Studio in Los Angeles. Vasun has been a visiting critic at Parsons The New School for Design, and taught Typography and Publication Design courses at Otis College of Art & Design and University of Southern California.
