An independent designer and educator with a broad array of collaborators in the sectors of architecture, culture, non-profits, museums and academia. Passionate about form, typography, storytelling and broad ideas. Vasun has worked with a range of studios such as Office of Paul Sahre, MoMA Design Studio, Ralph Appelbaum Associates in New York City, and Commonwealth Projects, IN-FO.CO and Imprint Projects in Los Angeles.

Since January 2019 Vasun has been collaborating closely with Public-Library on projects for Nike Rookie Diaries, Mac Miller, among many others.

Vasun received her MFA from Cranbrook Academy of Art in 2016 and her AAS from Parsons, The New School for Design. Her typographic work has been exhibited at the Beijing Design Festival and various platforms such as It’s Nice That, Ficciones Typografika and Women of Graphic Design. In January 2020 she completed a month long residency at AGA Lab in Amsterdam. She has been a visiting critic at Parsons The New School for Design, Lawrence Technological University and Santa Monica College, and taught Typography courses at Otis College of Art & Design.

She is currently an adjunct faculty member at Woodbury University teaching Typography & Environmental Graphics courses.
