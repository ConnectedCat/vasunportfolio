A designer and educator with a broad array of collaborators in the sectors of architecture, culture, non-profits, museums and academia. Passionate about form, typography, storytelling and broad ideas. She has collaborated with a range of studios such as Office of Paul Sahre, MoMA Design Studio, Ralph Appelbaum Associates in New York City, and more recently with Imprint Projects and Commonwealth Projects in Los Angeles.

She received her MFA from Cranbrook Academy of Art in 2016 and her AAS from Parsons, The New School for Design. Her typographic work has been exhibited at the 2015 Beijing Design Festival and various online publications such as It’s Nice That, Ficciones Typografika and Women of Graphic Design. Vasun has been a visiting critic at Parsons, The New School for Design in New York and Lawrence Technological University in Detroit. She is currently teaching typography and graphic design studio courses at Otis College of Art & Design and Santa Monica College.
